PolarBear

Polar Bears have black skin and although their fur appears white,it is actually transparent

  • Scientific name:Ursus Maritimus
  • Average Length:7.9-9.8ft
  • Average Lifespan:30 years of age
  • Habitat:Artic

Polar bears, also known as white bears, are truly massive carnivores native to the Arctic Circle in the northern hemisphere. These predators are known as hypercarnivores, which means that over 70% of their diet comes from meat.
